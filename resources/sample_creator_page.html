What is Bitpieces?

  • The main page of this site can give a more thorough description of what Bitpieces is about, but the general idea is this:
  • If you're a small business, content-creator, community project, or other startup, Bitpieces gives you a way to raise funding, by selling pieces of your brand, and giving automatic and continuous rewards to your funders. 
  • Bitpieces has 5 safety measures in place to protect funders, and make the experience safe and enjoyable.
  • The project is entirely open-source, hosted on github.
  • Learn more here.


What problem is Bitpieces trying to solve?

  • You're a content-creator who has a great idea for a project, but due to reasons outside of your control(maybe you have to work too much, or you have other financial obligations), you can't devote the necessary resources to working on your great idea. 
  • Getting funding for projects can be extremely difficult. Whether it's going to a bank to ask for a loan, or trying to get venture capital, the process can be excruciating.  

How does Bitpieces solve the problem of raising money?

  • By selling pieces of your brand, and promising monetary rewards to your funders over time. 
  • This is fundamentally different from other crowdfunding sites, which have a specified project goal.  Funders contribute to you or your brand, which may be doing many projects, all of which can be ongoing. They are willing to contribute because of the rewards they gain from owning pieces.
  • In Bitpieces, the rewards to your funders are immediate, automatic, continuous, and safe.
  • Selling pieces of your brand lets others see value in your ideas, and be a part of what you make and contribute to the world. 
  • Raising funds using Bitpieces is an easy 3-step process:
  • 1) Register here.
  • 2) Click the raise funding button. It will help you through the process of setting a reward amount, and deciding how much to sell your pieces for.
  • 3) Design your page, get verified, and encourage others to fund you.


Why Bitcoin?

  • As Bitcoin users, we try to be on the wavefront of new and emerging technologies and platforms. When we see something inventive or unique, we want to be a part of that as soon as possible, because usually the first adopters of potentially disruptive technologies and ideas are fiercely rewarded. 
  • Using Bitcoin ensures that funding is international. Bitpieces will fight fiercely to maintain its ability to be an international funding platform, and uses Bitcoin to avoid becoming mired in the potential regulations of any one specific country. Bitpieces exists on the web, and is made for web-users, anywhere in the world.

What is the reward for owning Bitpieces?

  • First and foremost, I want to reward my funders for supporting the project. This wouldn't be possible if they didn't see value in my efforts, and I want to thank them.
  • Bitpieces, in order to encourage others to do the same, is starting out with a generous reward yield, of 2%. This means that for every year, owning pieces of this brand will start out paying around $.01 per piece per year. If you guys decide that the pieces are valuable to own, then the value of the pieces may increase. Be wary of both overvaluing, and undervaluing the pieces.
  • The initial availability will be limited, so be aware of that.
  • My pledge to you, is to always be forthcoming, answer questions, and make the experience of using this site as great as it can be.
