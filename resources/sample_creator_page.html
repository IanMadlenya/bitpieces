Bitpieces


What is it?

  • The main page gives a more thorough description of what Bitpieces is about. If you're a small business, content-creator, community project, or other, Bitpieces gives you a way to raise funding, by selling pieces of your brand, and giving automatic and continuous rewards to your funders. 
  • Bitpieces has 5 safety measures in place to protect funders, and make the experience safe and enjoyable.
  • The project is entirely open-source, hosted on github.

  • Read through teh

What problem is Bitpieces trying to solve?

  • You're a content-creator who has a great idea for a project, but due to reasons outside of your control(maybe you have to work too much, or you have other financial obligations), you can't devote the necessary resources to working on your great idea. 
  • Getting funding for projects can be extremely difficult; whether it's going to a bank to ask for a loan, or trying to get venture capital, the process can be excruciating. Selling pieces of your brand lets others see value in your ideas, and be a part of your brand.

Why does Bitpieces need funds?

  • Right now, Bitpieces is made by me, Tyler Houlihan(bitpieces_dev1). If this project picks up, I can hire some more developers to contribute to the site, adding features, and making the user experience more enjoyable for you guys.
