What is Bitpieces?

Bitpieces is a crowd-funding platform that gives content creators, small businesses, and community projects a way to raise funding by selling pieces of their brand, and sharing profits with their funders through monetary rewards over time.



Register

Signing up is quick, easy, and free. Discover the creators you could potentially fund, or head over to our test site to learn the ropes, with no real money involved. If you'd like to get some Bitcoins in order to fund creators, head over to Coinbase. If you're a content-creator looking to raise some funds for your project, register here.

Buy pieces

Fund your favorite bands, artists, creators, and small businesses by buying pieces of their brand. Your pieces automatically and continuously earn rewards from the moment they are bought, which build up over time. After a creator has sold their initial pieces, users can bid and sell their pieces to other users on an open marketplace.

Earn rewards

Creators are required to pay rewards to their funders, starting out small, and growing as they grow their brand. This, along with a verification system, creator safety ratings, and a commenting system that provides a dialog between creators and their funders, builds a history of trust. Also, funders of projects earn badges and points for various objectives.

Fund creativity

A local band needs some equipment to go on the road, but doesn't have the cash. An educational youtuber needs to quit their job to make more videos. An artist needs supplies. A community organization is raising funds to throw events. Bitpieces gives content creators, small businesses, and community projects a way to raise funding by issuing pieces of their brand, and sharing profits with their funders through monetary rewards over time.

The world's first international funding platform

Using the bitcoin network as a universal payment processor ensures that anyone can fund who they want, anywhere in the world. Display the value of your pieces in any one of 20 currencies, on an interactive, funding dashboard. Fund projects in Visual Arts, Comics, Design, Dance, Education, Film and Video, Environment, Music, Fashion, Tech, Photography, Theatre, Food, Health, Writing and Lit, Sports, Small Business, Gaming, Crafts, and Journalism. Coinbase is used as the payment processor, which ensures free transactions within the bitcoin network.

Zero fees on a secure platform

Security is paramount for both funders and creators, which is why Bitpieces has a 5 pillar security strategy.

For funders, the experience of buying and trading pieces is entirely free. Similar to other funding sites, content-creators pay a 5% fee on withdrawals. When not using coinbase, your client may include network fees when you first buy pieces.

Open source funding

The source code of the entire project is open-source, hosted on github. In addition, an open trading API is in development, to allow funders to buy and sell pieces programmatically.
