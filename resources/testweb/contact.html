Contact

For bugs, feature requests, or any issues with the website, create a new issue on the github repository.

For all inquiries about making an account and raising funds, send an email to bitpieces22@gmail.com

For verifying your creator account, send an email to bitpieces22verify@gmail.com with either:
  • A picture of you, your content, and your bitpieces creator name.
  • A link to a post or a page from your website or youtube channel, containing your creator name.
